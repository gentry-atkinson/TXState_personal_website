Team Bobcat



Located in San Marcos, Team Bobcat is proud to compete in combat robotics throughout Texas. We were founded in September of 2019 and are proud to represent the highest standards of design, construction, and operation for fighting robots. Our strength is the depth of knowledge present in the students and alumni of Texas State University. Combat Robotics presents challenges that beyond those in any other building or engineering competition. We welcome those challenges!
